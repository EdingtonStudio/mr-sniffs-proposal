05 · The early lean

Giard gets paid first.
Edington gets paid back.

Here's the heart of it. Edington took the cash risk, Giard is taking the sweat risk. So during the recoup phase, distributable profit leans 70/30 toward Giard until Giard has banked the Build Pool, default 1.5x the Advance. The Advance repays out of Edington's 30% the whole time, in parallel. Neither side waits behind the other. Then it flips to 50/50 for good.

Long-stop: 30 months

If the pool isn't full by month 30, the lean ends anyway and the split goes 50/50. It cannot run forever.

Then 50/50, for good

Once the pool is banked, the split reverts to even and never leans again.

Optional kicker

One-time bonus to Giard if a stretch revenue milestone clears during recoup. Yes or no is an open item.

08 · Protections

Boring on purpose.

Plain language now, lawyer language later. These protect both sides, in both directions.

Vesting

Both equity stakes vest over 3 years with a 1-year cliff. Nobody walks early with half the brand.

IP + domain

Brand, IP and Mrsniffs.co are assigned to the LLC when it forms, with reversion to Edington Studio if the brand winds down.

Reserved matters

Big moves like new debt, dilution, or selling the brand need Edington's sign-off. Day-to-day stays with Giard.

Non-compete

Neither partner builds a competing incense brand while in, and for an agreed period after.

Buy-sell

Drag-along and tag-along rights. If a real buyer shows up, neither side can block or get left behind.

Deadlock

Structured resolution before anything nuclear: cool-off, mediation, then the buy-sell mechanics.
